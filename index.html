About Us

Our society at NetaJi Subhas University Of Technology is renowned for its hard work in designing and building All-Terrain-Vehicles, also known as Buggies. We compete in prestigious ATV competitions for students, such as BAJA SAEINDIA and ATVC. Our team's dedication and exceptional engineering skills have led us to achieve great success in these competitions. We take pride in our ability to overcome challenges and produce high-quality vehicles that can withstand tough terrains. Our Captain and Vice Captain provide invaluable guidance and leadership, which has been critical to our success. Overall, we strive to continue pushing boundaries and making a mark in the world of ATV design and engineering.
